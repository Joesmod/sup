RESPONSE:

The city of Chicago is pulling the office of the mayor out of the mayor's office and creating a separate office with various responsibilities as outlined in the management ordinance. There are 10 positions budgeted for this office which includes the chief sustainability officer, grants research specialists, and project managers. There is a division within the office of the mayor that will serve as the director of veteran affairs. There are also four positions added to the homeless services division within the Department of Family Support Services, as well as a director position at DFS that will lead the assistance for the city's new arrivals initiative. The budget for animal care and control advertising is increased, as well as hours for the general laborers. The AARP grant funds are amended by providing the Department of Family and Support Services funding to increase rapid rehousing capacity from 800 units to 1200 units for 2023, as well as 500,000 for low barrier shelters. Various technical adjustments and corrections are made in various departments.
